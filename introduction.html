My Frontend Journey

My development journey began with learning Full Stack Web Development using Python at the Azad Chaiwala Institute (Lahore Branch). While the experience gave me valuable backend knowledge, I realized after some time—especially during my job search—that I needed to strengthen my frontend skills first.

I made a strategic decision to focus completely on Frontend Development, building a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like Tailwind CSS, React JS. This shift has allowed me to understand UI/UX design principles better and create responsive, modern web interfaces that deliver real value.

It's now been over one year since I started focusing on frontend development, and I’ve grown confident in building clean, dynamic, and user-friendly websites. My next goal is to return to backend development—with a much stronger frontend base—so I can eventually work as a complete full stack developer.
